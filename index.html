DOGECOIN

Dogecoin is primarily the unintentional cryptocurrency revolution that brings joy to people! It is also a peer-to-peer cryptocurrency that is open source and uses blockchain technology, which is a very safe decentralized method of keeping data in a public ledger that is kept up to date by a network of computers known as nodes. But more than this is Dogecoin's philosophy, which is summed out in the Dogecoin Manifesto, and its wonderful, active community, which is made up of kind people just like you! Find out more.
